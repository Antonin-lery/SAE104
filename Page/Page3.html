My study project

My professional project

I don’t know yet but for the moment my choice leans more towards the job of pentester. That’s why I want to take the cybersecurity option because the job of pentester requires solid knowledge in network and IT security. I think this job suits me because I am patient, I like the world around cybersecurity and I have a sense of impeccable ethics.

However, if after the IUT I do not have the ability and desire to continue my studies, I think I would make network administrator. In addition, I would love to work in the field of cybersecurity within the army to help my country as much as possible .
